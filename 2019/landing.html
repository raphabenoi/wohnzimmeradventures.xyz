Wohnzimmer Adventures (WZA) is a special gathering occurring every year before Christmas. Our living-rooms are re-imagined as a platform for different activities created by the participants. Ranging from “pasta bridge building” to “animal sounds in the living room”, an array of workshops, performances, concerts and parties create a unique ambiance for meeting other people and having fun. Engineers, artists, designers, consultants, accountants… : all colors, genders and careers are welcome to propose an idea or come along and enjoy.

where

WZA takes place in people's livingrooms as well as party places in Berlin. A detailed schedule will be communicated before the event.
