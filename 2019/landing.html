Wohnzimmeradventures #wza is a friends-festival happening in several Berliner swimming pools – äh… living rooms! For 3,3 days, you can join us in inventing, realizing and participating in an array of activities, performances, concerts and parties, creating a unique ambiance for meeting other people, learning from and with them and having fun. All are welcome in their own beauty and wisdom. So pack your hottest Adiletten, don your best swimsuit, buy your season pass, and don’t forget the sunscreen – got everything? Great, then join us, because this year it’s pool party time!

WZA takes place in people's livingrooms as well as party places in Berlin. A detailed schedule will be communicated before the event.
