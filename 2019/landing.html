Wohnzimmeradventures #wza is a special festival happening in several Berliner living rooms! For four days, you can join us in inventing, realizing and participating in an array of activities, performances, concerts and parties, creating a unique ambiance for meeting other people, learning from and with them and having fun.

All colors, genders and careers are welcome to propose an idea or come along and enjoy.

So pack your hottest Adiletten, don your best swimsuit, buy the admission ticket, and don’t forget the sunscreen – got everything?
Great, then join us, because this year it’s pool party time!
