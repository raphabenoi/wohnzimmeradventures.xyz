Trainingsplan

A body immersed up to the neck in water only weighs 1/10 of its weight! This fantastic feature allows almost anyone regardless of age, sex and fitness to train in the water with very little risk of injury. Therefore, our attractive and varied aqua fitness Trainingsplan definitely offers the right course for you to feel physically healthy, fit and balanced.

So that everyone can have the greatest possible health benefits and at the same time have fun in the water, we offer training in several different stress levels. More information on this can be found in the respective detailed course descriptions.
