  • 3 pm

    Construction of Masculinity

    #discussion — duration: 2.5 hours

    waldemars_SALON

    In this workshop we want to reflect and discuss about masculinity. Men's liberation from the socio-cultural constraints of sexism and gender roles is a necessary part of feminist activism, for that reason it is important to create possibilities and spaces to become aware of typical male behaviors in our own behavior and why we behave like, as well as what is necessary to behave in a different way.
    First we will try to focus on our own personal points of contact to the topic, our perception and experience with role expectations and how we feel or are dependent on socio-cultural constraints.
    Then we will try to reflect our own emotions concerning this topic: fears, hopes, insecurities, injuries.
    We will try to create a space where people feel free to talk about their insecurities, their feelings of anger, shame and concern.
    This workshop is open for all genders. However, we might split the group for a period of time to allow people to reflect in a more open and honest way, without feeling judged, sharing afterwards the conclusions and emotions.

    Host: Martin

    Capacity: 20 people
